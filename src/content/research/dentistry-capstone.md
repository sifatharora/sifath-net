For my research capstone, I explored the intersection of life science and dentistry — looking at how biological principles apply to oral health and dental care.

## Project overview

The project involved researching how cellular biology, microbiology, and anatomy connect to common dental procedures and preventive care. I presented my findings in a final contact contest format, focusing on making complex science accessible to a general audience.

## Results

- **3rd Place** in the Life Science & Dentistry Research Capstone Project
- **Winner** of the Research Capstone Final Contact Contest

## What I learned

This project reinforced my interest in dentistry as a career that sits at the intersection of science and patient care. Presenting research also helped me develop my public speaking and communication skills — something I value from my Destination Imagination experience as well.

I'm planning to build on this work with more research projects throughout high school.
